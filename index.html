About Me

I am Thathsarani Navodya, a second-year undergraduate pursuing a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology (Hons) at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Faculty of Technology.

I am passionate about the field of ICT and continuously working to develop my knowledge and skills through learning and practical experiences. I have worked as a tutor for A/L ICT students and served as the Co-Head of the paper panel under Prasath Wickramasinha, which has strengthened my subject knowledge, leadership, and communication skills. In addition, I work part-time as a promoter, where I have developed strong interpersonal, communication, and teamwork skills.

I am currently exploring different areas within ICT to identify my specialization, with the goal of building a successful career and contributing meaningfully to the ICT industry in Sri Lanka.
